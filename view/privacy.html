Privacy Policy

We collect personal information when you make a booking or contact us. This includes your name, email address, phone number, and payment details.

Your information is used to process your bookings, provide customer support, and send you updates related to your reservation.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except when necessary to process your booking or as required by law.

4. Data Security

We take the security of your personal data seriously and use industry-standard encryption methods to protect it.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Please contact us if you wish to make any changes to your data.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. The date of the most recent update will be shown at the bottom of this page.

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at any time.
